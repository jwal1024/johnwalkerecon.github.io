title: "General Equilibrium Effects of Universal Cash Transfers: Experimental Evidence At-Scale from Malawi"
collection: work-in-progress

In joint work with Dennis Egger, Winnie Mughogo and Tilman Graff we trace out the high-frequency spatial general equilibrium effects of large, universal, and unconditional cash transfers to approx. 100k beneficiaries, amounting to 125% of GDP.
